FAQs

What is RPM?

RPM (Revenue Per Mille) is how much you earn per 1000 views after YouTube cuts.

What affects RPM?

Your niche, country, audience type, and advertiser demand affect RPM.

Is this income before or after YouTube's cut?

This calculator shows your earnings after YouTube takes its 45% cut.
