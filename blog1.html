10 Tips to Save AC Electricity Bill

Published on: July 1, 2025

1. Set AC Temperature to 24-26°C

Higher temperature settings save more energy compared to 18°C or 20°C.

2. Use Energy-Efficient AC

Switch to inverter ACs or 5-star rated models for huge savings.

3. Seal Air Leaks

Make sure doors and windows are closed properly to prevent cool air leakage.

4. Clean AC Filters Regularly

Dirty filters force AC to work harder, increasing electricity usage.

5. Use Ceiling Fans

Fans help circulate cool air better, reducing AC load.

6. Switch Off When Not in Use

Don’t leave your AC on when you're not in the room.

7. Install Curtains/Blinds

Block direct sunlight to reduce room heat load.

8. Service AC Annually

Regular servicing keeps your AC efficient and saves power.

9. Use Timer Mode

Set auto-off during the night or when cooling is no longer needed.

10. Switch to Solar

If possible, power your AC with solar energy for zero electricity cost.

Conclusion: Follow these tips and you’ll see a noticeable drop in your AC electricity bills this summer.
