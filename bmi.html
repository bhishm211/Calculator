FAQs

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) indicates whether your weight is healthy according to your height.

What is a healthy BMI?

A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy.

Does BMI apply to all?

BMI is a general guideline and may not be accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or highly muscular people.
