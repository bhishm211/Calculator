Pregnancy Care Guide - Month by Month

Published: July 1, 2025

First Trimester (1-3 Months)

✔️ Focus on folic acid, regular checkups, avoid alcohol, and eat nutrient-rich food.

Second Trimester (4-6 Months)

✔️ Mild exercise, iron-rich food, and regular fetal checkups become crucial.

Third Trimester (7-9 Months)

✔️ More rest, monitor baby movements, prepare for delivery, and consult your doctor frequently.

General Tips:

  • Eat balanced diet
  • Stay hydrated
  • Avoid stress
  • Regular doctor visits

Conclusion: Healthy lifestyle and regular checkups are key to a safe and smooth pregnancy journey.
