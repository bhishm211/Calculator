5 Biggest Loan Mistakes to Avoid

Published: July 1, 2025

1. Not Comparing Loan Offers

People often take the first loan offer they get without comparing interest rates, fees, and repayment terms. This can cost you thousands.

2. Ignoring the Fine Print

Not reading the terms leads to hidden fees, penalties, and surprises during repayment.

3. Borrowing More Than Needed

Borrowing extra money leads to higher EMIs and unnecessary interest payments.

4. Not Checking Credit Score

Your credit score impacts your interest rate. A poor score means expensive loans.

5. No Prepayment Planning

Many people don’t consider prepayment options. Paying off early can save a lot in interest.

Conclusion: Be a smart borrower — compare offers, read terms, borrow wisely, and plan repayments efficiently.
