FAQs

How is the due date calculated?

It's calculated by adding 280 days (40 weeks) to the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP).

Is this due date always accurate?

It provides an estimate. Actual delivery may vary by a couple of weeks earlier or later.

Can I use this for IVF or other pregnancies?

This is designed for natural conception. IVF due dates can differ slightly.
