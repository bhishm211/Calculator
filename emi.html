FAQs

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the monthly payment for a loan that includes both principal and interest.

How is EMI calculated?

It depends on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure using a standard EMI formula.

Can I reduce my EMI?

Yes, by increasing the tenure or choosing a lower interest rate.
