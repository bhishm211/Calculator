How Much Does YouTube Pay in 2025?

Published: July 1, 2025

What is RPM and CPM?

RPM is Revenue Per 1000 views (after YouTube’s cut). CPM is the amount advertisers pay per 1000 ad views (before YouTube’s cut).

Average RPM in 2025:

  • India: ₹20 – ₹180
  • USA: ₹400 – ₹900
  • UK: ₹300 – ₹750

Factors Affecting RPM:

  • Audience country
  • Niche (Finance, Tech, Gaming)
  • Video length (8+ mins earn more)
  • Ad engagement

Example Income:

1 million views from USA at ₹600 RPM = ₹6,00,000. From India at ₹100 RPM = ₹1,00,000.

Conclusion: YouTube income highly depends on niche, country, and video type. Focus on high RPM niches like finance, tech, and education.
