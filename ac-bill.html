How the AC Bill Calculator Works

This calculator estimates your air conditioning electricity costs based on:

  1. AC Power Consumption: Measured in kilowatts (kW) based on AC tonnage
  2. Usage Duration: How many hours per day and days per month you use the AC
  3. Electricity Rate: Your local cost per kilowatt-hour (kWh)
  4. AC Type: Regular or more efficient inverter models

The formula used is: Cost = Power (kW) × Hours × Days × Rate × Efficiency Factor

For more accurate results, check your AC's exact power rating (usually found on the nameplate) and your electricity bill for the exact rate you pay.
